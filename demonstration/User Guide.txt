How to use this Simulator !!!!
-------------------------------
Step 1: 
Enter the total number of transactions that operates concurrently within a schedule.

For example: 5 (T1, T2, T3, T4, T5)

Step 2:
Enter the total number of variables the transactions will use.

For example: 4 (A, B, C, D)

Step 3:
Click the "Start" button and 
enter the sequence of operations of this schedule.

Step 4:
Click the "Generate" button to generate the Precedence graph.

Step 5:
Click the "Check Serializability" button to check the conflict serializability and serial order (if exists).

For serial schedule, it will return the serial order.
For non-serial schedule, it will return the cycle.


Thank you.
